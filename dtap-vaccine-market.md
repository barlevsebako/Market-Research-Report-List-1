<p><ul><li>Inactivated Vaccine</li><li>Live Attenuated Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DTaP vaccine market includes two main types of vaccines: inactivated and live attenuated. Inactivated vaccines contain killed pathogens, offering immunity without the risk of disease. This type is widely used due to its safety profile and effectiveness. Live attenuated vaccines use weakened forms of the pathogen, providing a more robust immune response. However, they carry a slight risk of causing disease in immunocompromised individuals. Both types play crucial roles in preventing diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis.</p></p>
