<p><strong>The Vacuum Casting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Non-centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum casting machine market can be categorized into two main types: centrifugal and non-centrifugal. Centrifugal vacuum casting utilizes rotational force to distribute molten material into molds, ensuring uniform filling and reducing defects, making it ideal for intricate designs. Non-centrifugal vacuum casting, on the other hand, relies on atmospheric pressure and vacuum techniques to draw the material into the mold, providing high precision for simple shapes and small-run productions. Each type serves distinct applications based on project requirements.</p></p>
