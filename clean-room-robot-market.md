<p><p>The clean room robot market encompasses various robot types designed for precise and contamination-free tasks. Articulated robots offer flexibility with multiple joints for complex movements. SCARA robots excel in assembly and packaging with high-speed, horizontal motions. Parallel robots provide exceptional precision and speed, ideal for delicate operations. Cartesian robots use linear movements along three axes for straightforward tasks. Collaborative robots are designed to work safely alongside humans, enhancing productivity in clean environments by performing repetitive or hazardous tasks.</p></p>
