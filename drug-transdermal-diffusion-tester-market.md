<p><p>The Drug Transdermal Diffusion Tester Market is categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on the method of diffusion testing. Vertical testers analyze drug diffusion through a vertical membrane, simulating skin application and allowing gravity-driven flow, ideal for studying penetration rates. Horizontal testers, conversely, facilitate drug diffusion across a horizontal surface, focusing on more uniform distribution and dynamics. Both types are essential for assessing drug formulations’ efficacy and skin permeability, guiding product development in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
