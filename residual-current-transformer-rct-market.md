<p><p>Residual Current Transformers (RCTs) are crucial for detecting electrical imbalances and ensuring safety in electrical systems. The market for RCTs is categorized into three types: circular type, rectangle type, and open type. Circular types, often compact, are designed for easy integration in existing installations. Rectangle types provide a larger surface area, suitable for higher capacities. Open types, featuring a split-core design, allow for retrofitting around existing cables, making them ideal for upgrades without extensive system modifications. Each type serves distinct applications based on installation needs.</p></p>
