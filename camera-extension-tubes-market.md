<p><p>Camera extension tubes are used to increase the distance between the camera lens and the image sensor, allowing for closer macro photography. The market is divided into two main types: automatic and manual extension tubes. Automatic extension tubes feature electronic connections that enable communication between the camera and lens, preserving autofocus and exposure settings. Both types serve the needs of macro photographers, offering varying levels of convenience and control.</p></p>
