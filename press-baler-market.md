<p><p>The press baler market features two primary types: horizontal and vertical baling machines. Horizontal balers efficiently compress materials like plastic, metal, and paper into dense bales for transport and recycling, making them ideal for high-volume operations. Vertical balers, on the other hand, suit smaller operations, producing bales from materials such as cardboard and plastic, and are often more space-efficient. Both types play critical roles in waste management, recycling, and resource recovery across various industries.</p></p>
