<p><p>The Vascular Self-Expanding Stent System is a medical device designed to support and maintain the patency of blood vessels, particularly in cases of vascular diseases such as atherosclerosis. Additionally, the integration of drug-eluting technologies into stent designs is emerging, aimed at preventing restenosis. Increased investment in research and development, along with a rise in minimally invasive surgical procedures, further supports the market's expansion.
