<p><strong>The Blower Conveying System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Drive</li><li>Belt Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blower conveying system market is primarily categorized into direct drive and belt drive types. Direct drive systems utilize a motor directly connected to the blower, offering higher efficiency and reduced maintenance due to fewer moving parts. In contrast, belt drive systems employ belts to connect the motor to the blower, providing flexibility in installation and the potential for speed adjustment. Each type serves distinct applications based on requirements for efficiency, maintenance, and operational flexibility in material handling.</p></p>
