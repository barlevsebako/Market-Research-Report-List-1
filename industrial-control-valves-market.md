<p><ul><li>3-way</li><li>2-way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial control valves market primarily consists of two key types: 2-way and 3-way valves. </p><p>2-way valves are designed to control the flow of fluids in a single direction, either fully opening or fully closing, making them suitable for on-off applications. In contrast, 3-way valves can direct flow between two outlets or enable mixing and diverting processes, providing more complex flow control. These valves are essential in various industries for regulating pressure, flow, and temperature in piping systems.</p></p>
