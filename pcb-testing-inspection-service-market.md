<p><p>PCB testing and inspection services encompass various methods to ensure the reliability and functionality of printed circuit boards. X-ray testing enables inspection of internal structures like solder joints. The flying probe test uses movable probes for fast electrical testing without customized fixtures. Functional testing evaluates the complete performance of a PCB in a working state. In-circuit testing (ICT) checks individual components for accuracy and functionality. Other methods may include visual inspections or automated optical inspections, catering to diverse testing needs.</p></p>
