<p><p>The non-contact multi-function infrared thermometer market includes two primary types: forehead and ear thermometers. Forehead thermometers measure temperature from the skin's surface using infrared sensors, providing quick results and minimizing cross-contamination risks. Ear thermometers, on the other hand, use infrared technology to gauge temperature from the ear canal, delivering accurate readings typically in less time than traditional methods. Both types are widely used in clinical settings, households, and during health screenings, driven by the need for efficient and hygienic temperature assessments.</p></p>
