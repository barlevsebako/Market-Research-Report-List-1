<p><strong>The Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Resistors</li><li>Non-Linear Resistors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resistors market can be categorized into linear and non-linear resistors. Linear resistors have a constant resistance value, meaning their voltage-current relationship follows Ohm's law, making them widely used in circuits for predictable performance. In contrast, non-linear resistors exhibit resistance that varies with voltage or current changes, leading to applications in specialized electronic components like thermistors and varistors. These different resistor types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics, each requiring specific electrical characteristics.</p></p>
