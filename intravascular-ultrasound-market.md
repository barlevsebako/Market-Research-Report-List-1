<p><p>The intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) market comprises three main segments: accessories, IVUS consoles, and IVUS catheters. Accessories include guide wires and sheaths that support IVUS procedures. IVUS consoles are the imaging systems that process and display ultrasound data, essential for interpretation. IVUS catheters, which are inserted into blood vessels, contain the ultrasound transducer that provides real-time imaging of vascular structures. Together, these components enhance diagnostic accuracy and aid in various vascular interventions, driving growth in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
