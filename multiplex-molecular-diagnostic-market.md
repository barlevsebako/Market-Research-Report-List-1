<p><strong>The Multiplex Molecular Diagnostic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Real-time PCR</li><li>Nested PCR with Melt Curve Analysis</li><li>PCR with Low density Microarray</li><li>PCR with Liquid Phase Bead Array</li><li>PCR with Electrochemical Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multiplex molecular diagnostic market encompasses various techniques for simultaneous detection of multiple targets. Real-time PCR provides quantitative analysis using fluorescent probes. Nested PCR with melt curve analysis enhances sensitivity and specificity through a two-step amplification process followed by temperature-based differentiation. PCR with low-density microarrays enables the analysis of multiple targets on a single chip, while PCR with liquid phase bead arrays utilizes beads for target capture and detection. PCR with electrochemical detection employs electrochemical sensors for real-time monitoring, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
