<p><ul><li>Off-grid</li><li>Off-grid with Grid Support</li><li>Grid-tied</li><li>Grid-tied with Battery Back Up</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential solar power systems can be categorized into several types based on their connectivity and support. Off-grid systems operate independently, relying solely on stored solar energy, ideal for remote areas. Off-grid with grid support systems combine off-grid capabilities with grid access, allowing for backup power. Grid-tied systems connect directly to the utility grid, enabling homeowners to sell excess energy. Lastly, grid-tied with battery backup systems offer the benefits of grid connectivity along with stored energy for use during outages, enhancing reliability.</p></p>
