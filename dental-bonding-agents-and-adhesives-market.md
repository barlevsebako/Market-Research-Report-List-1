<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dental Bonding Agents and Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total-etch Bonding Agent</li><li>Self-etch Bonding Agent</li><li>Universal Bonding Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental bonding agents and adhesives market is categorized into three main types. Total-etch bonding agents require etching of both enamel and dentin before application, enhancing adhesion but involving multiple steps. Self-etch bonding agents simplify the process by combining etching and priming, making them easier to use while still providing effective bonding. Universal bonding agents offer versatility, allowing use with various protocols, including total-etch and self-etch techniques, appealing to a broad range of dental procedures and preferences.</p></p>
