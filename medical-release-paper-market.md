<p><p>The medical release paper market is divided into two primary types: single-sided coated and double-sided coated papers. Single-sided coated paper features a coating on one side, primarily used for applications requiring adhesion on the coated surface, such as bandages or adhesive dressings. In contrast, double-sided coated paper is coated on both sides, enabling versatility for applications needing adhesive properties on both surfaces, like some types of surgical tapes. Both types cater to specific medical needs, enhancing functionality in healthcare settings.</p></p>
