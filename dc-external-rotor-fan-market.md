<p><p>The DC external rotor fan market comprises three primary types: axial flow fans, centrifugal fans, and others. Axial flow fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, offering efficient cooling in compact spaces, commonly found in HVAC systems. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, utilize rotational energy to move air at right angles, providing higher pressure and flow, suitable for dust removal and industrial applications. The "other" category includes specialty fans designed for specific tasks or environments, enhancing versatility in various applications.</p></p>
