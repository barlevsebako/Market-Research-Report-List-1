<p><strong>The Snow Melting Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Regulating Type</li><li>Constant Wattage Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snow melting cable market primarily comprises two types: self-regulating and constant wattage cables. Self-regulating cables automatically adjust their heat output based on ambient temperatures, ensuring energy efficiency and preventing overheating. In contrast, constant wattage cables provide a fixed level of heat regardless of external conditions, offering consistent performance but potentially higher energy consumption. Both types are widely used in various applications, including driveways, walkways, and roofs, to combat snow and ice accumulation, enhancing safety and convenience.</p></p>
