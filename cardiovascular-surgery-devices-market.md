<p><p>The cardiovascular surgery devices market encompasses various technologies essential for cardiac procedures. Beating heart surgery systems enable surgeons to operate on a heart still in motion, enhancing safety and recovery. Cardiopulmonary bypass equipment temporarily takes over heart and lung functions, allowing for intricate operations. Cardiac ablation devices target and disrupt abnormal electrical pathways in the heart to treat arrhythmias. Perfusion disposables are crucial consumables used during surgery to maintain blood flow and oxygenation, ensuring optimal patient outcomes.</p></p>
