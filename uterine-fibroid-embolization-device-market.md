<p><p>The Uterine Fibroid Embolization Device market includes various types of embolic agents used to obstruct blood flow to fibroids. Gelatin sponge is a natural, absorbable material that provides temporary occlusion and eventual resorption. Polyvinyl alcohol particles are synthetic, non-absorbable agents that create permanent embolization. Trisacryl gelatin microspheres provide controlled particle sizes for effective blockage, while polymethyl methacrylate microspheres are rigid and permanent, promoting long-lasting embolization. Each type offers unique characteristics, catering to different clinical needs in uterine fibroid treatment.</p></p>
