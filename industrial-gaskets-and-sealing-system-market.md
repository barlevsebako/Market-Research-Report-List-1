<p><p>The industrial gaskets and sealing system market is categorized into three main types: non-metallic, semi-metallic, and metallic gaskets. Non-metallic gaskets, made from materials like rubber, PTFE, and foam, offer excellent sealing in low-pressure environments. Semi-metallic gaskets combine materials such as graphite and metal to provide enhanced durability and resistance to extreme temperatures and pressures. Metallic gaskets, crafted from metals like steel and copper, are designed for high-pressure and high-temperature applications, offering robust sealing solutions in demanding industrial settings.</p></p>
