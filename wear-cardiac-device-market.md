<p><p>The wearable cardiac device market includes various types such as defibrillators, patch monitors, and Holter monitors. Defibrillators deliver electrical shocks to restore normal heart rhythm during emergencies. Patch monitors are lightweight, adhesive devices that continuously track heart rhythms and vital signs, providing real-time data for remote monitoring. Holter monitors, usually worn for 24-48 hours, record heart activity over time, helping doctors diagnose arrhythmias and other cardiac conditions. Together, these devices enhance patient care and enable proactive heart health management.</p></p>
