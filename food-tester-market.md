<p><p>The food tester market comprises several key types, each serving distinct functions. Spectrometers analyze food composition by measuring light absorption, while refractometers assess sugar levels and concentrations through light refraction. Titrators determine the chemical content of food by adding reagents to a sample until a reaction endpoint is reached. Moisture analyzers quantify water content, which is crucial for quality control. Additionally, "Others" encompasses various specialized instruments, including pH meters and texture analyzers, enhancing the quality assurance processes in the food industry.</p></p>
