<p><p>The plastic cling film market encompasses various types, including polyethylene (PE), polyvinyl chloride (PVC), polyvinylidene chloride (PVDC), and poly-methylpentene (PMP). PE cling films are popular for food wrapping due to their flexibility and adherence. PVC films offer durability and are often used in food packaging. PVDC provides excellent barrier properties, making it ideal for sensitive products. PMP is lightweight and exhibits high heat resistance, suitable for specialized applications. Each type serves distinct needs in packaging and preservation.</p></p>
