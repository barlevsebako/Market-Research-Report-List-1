<p><p>The fruit tree shaker market is categorized into three main types: mounted, self-propelled, and trailed. Mounted shakers are attached to tractors, offering flexibility and ease of use but requiring a compatible base machine. Self-propelled shakers are standalone units, providing enhanced mobility and maneuverability, making them suitable for various orchard sizes. Trailed shakers connect to vehicles and are designed for larger operations, allowing efficient harvesting over extensive areas while maintaining stability and power. Each type caters to different operational needs in fruit harvesting.</p></p>
