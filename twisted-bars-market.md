<p><strong>The Twisted Bars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-rolling</li><li>Cold Rolling</li><li>Cold Drawing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Twisted bars are categorized into three primary market types based on processing methods. Hot-rolling involves shaping metal at high temperatures, enhancing ductility and reducing strength, making it suitable for construction applications. Cold rolling occurs at room temperature, resulting in a smoother finish and greater tensile strength, ideal for precision components. Cold drawing further refines the material by pulling it through a die, producing accurate dimensions and improved mechanical properties, making it preferred for high-strength applications in various industries.</p></p>
