<p><p>The organic manure market can be categorized into two main types: animal-based concentrated organic manures and plant-based concentrated organic manures. Animal-based manures are derived from livestock, such as cattle, poultry, and pig waste, offering high nutrient content and beneficial microorganisms. In contrast, plant-based manures are sourced from decomposed plant materials, like crop residues and green manures, providing essential nutrients and improving soil structure. Both types enhance soil fertility, promote sustainable agriculture, and reduce reliance on chemical fertilizers.</p></p>
