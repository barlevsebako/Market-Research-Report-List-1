<p><ul><li>Vertical Auger Model</li><li>Horizontal Auger Model</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Masticating juicers are categorized into two primary market types: vertical auger models and horizontal auger models. Vertical auger juicers feature a compact design, allowing for easy operation and quick assembly, ideal for small kitchens. They excel at juicing leafy greens and soft fruits. In contrast, horizontal auger juicers are larger and more versatile, providing a higher juice yield and better efficiency for hard fruits and vegetables. Both models utilize an auger mechanism to crush produce, preserving nutrients and flavor.</p></p>
