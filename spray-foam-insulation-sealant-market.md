<p><p>Spray foam insulation sealants are categorized into three main types based on density: high, medium, and low. High-density foam offers superior insulation and structural support, making it ideal for commercial applications and areas exposed to heavy loads. Medium-density foam provides a balance of insulation and flexibility, suitable for residential and light commercial use. Low-density foam is lightweight and cost-effective, commonly used for unconditioned spaces like attics, offering basic insulation and air sealing properties. Each type serves distinct insulation needs and applications.</p></p>
