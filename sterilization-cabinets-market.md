<p><p>Sterilization cabinets are essential in healthcare and laboratory settings for ensuring equipment and instruments are free from microorganisms. The market can be categorized into two main types: dry heat sterilization cabinets, which utilize high temperatures to eliminate pathogens without moisture, making them ideal for heat-stable items; and damp and heat sterilization cabinets, also known as autoclaves, which combine steam and heat under pressure, effectively sterilizing a wide range of materials, including those sensitive to dry heat. Each type serves distinct sterilization needs.</p></p>
