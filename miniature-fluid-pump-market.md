<p><p>The miniature fluid pump market encompasses various types, each suited for specific applications. Diaphragm pumps use flexible membranes to move fluids, offering low pulsation and good chemical compatibility. Peristaltic pumps operate by compressing flexible tubing, ensuring a clean fluid path ideal for sterile applications. Piston pumps utilize a reciprocating mechanism to deliver precise fluid volumes, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Other types include gear pumps and screw pumps, catering to specialized needs in various industries, including medical, automotive, and analytical equipment.</p></p>
