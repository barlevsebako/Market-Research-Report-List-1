<p><p>Vertical farming encompasses various market types including hydroponics, aeroponics, and other innovative cultivation methods. Aeroponics, on the other hand, relies on misting plant roots with nutrient solutions, maximizing oxygen exposure and efficiency. Other methods may include aquaponics, which combines fish farming with plant growth.
