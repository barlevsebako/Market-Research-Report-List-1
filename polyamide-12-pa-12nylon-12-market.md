<p><p>Polyamide 12 (PA 12), commonly known as Nylon 12, exhibits two primary market types based on its synthesis methods: polycondensation and ring-opening polymerization. The polycondensation method involves the reaction of diamines and dicarboxylic acids, resulting in long polymer chains with excellent mechanical properties. In contrast, the ring-opening polymerization method utilizes cyclic monomers, favoring lower moisture absorption and enhanced dimensional stability. These processes cater to diverse applications in automotive, electronics, and medical sectors, enhancing performance and versatility.</p></p>
